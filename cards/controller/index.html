Controller Card

List of articles related to the 'Controller' card

The role of the controller card is to operate the various control lines of the computer at the right time according to the current instruction class. It makes up a key part of the broader ‘control unit’ within the computer. It is primarily driven by the sequencer card to know ‘when’ something should happen.
