ALU Arithmetic Unit Card

List of articles related to the 'ALU Arithmetic Unit' card

The Arithmetic Unit is one of the three cards making up the broader Artihmetic Logic Unit (ALU). This card primarily handles 8-bit addition operations (adding together the inputs from register B and C) but can also perform an 8-bit increment (adding 1 to the input from register B). In either case the output from this card can then be loaded into register A or D.
