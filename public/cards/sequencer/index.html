Sequencer Card

List of articles related to the 'Sequencer' card

The sequencer card is effectively a Finite State Machine (FSM) and makes up the broader ‘control unit’ within the computer. This card’s role is to produce a series of pulses at specified times that the controller card can then use to operate the various control lines of the computer at the right time.
