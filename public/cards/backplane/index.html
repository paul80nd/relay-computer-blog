Backplane Cards

List of articles related to the 'Backplane' cards

Not a single card but actually one of four … and technically not a card at all but they are card shaped. These are the ‘cards’ that connect a group of five cards together in each card bay (W, X, Y and Z). Each carries a selection of lines and busses to the cards it holds and also hosts connections to the ribbon cables taking the busses and control lines of to other backplane cards and areas of the computer.
